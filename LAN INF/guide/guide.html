GUIDE

> Welcome in LAN Infiltrator game!

> Your task is to infiltrate a virus into a small company's network.

> The company has 9 rooms and you will win the game only if every room is infected.

> To decode the key try to write the key backwards.

> The color of the room shows its status:

  • Green - Infected
  • Blue - Not Infected
  • Red - Secured (Firewall Active)

> If your detection level reaches 100%, you have lost the game.

> ALWAYS THINK BEFORE YOU DO ANYTHING.

> Have fun!
